About Us

Rain and Angel Summer Paradise and Beach Resort was a beautiful beach that stretched along the coastline. It used to be a busy port where big ships came and went. Covering a big area of 3000 square meters, this coastal sanctuary showed how beautiful nature can be. This beach was also known before for its sea turtles, which swam gracefully in the clear water.

Rain and Angel summer paradise and beach resort is Surrounded by beautiful ocean views and lush greenery and an ideal getaway from the busyness of everyday life. A welcoming retreat for couples seeking relaxation or individuals and families in search of gentle moments and cherished memories. Experience pure relaxation With our comfort facilities, tasty food, and lots of fun activities.
